🥣 3-Ingredient Banana Pancakes 🥞

Healthy-ish, fluffy & no blender needed — breakfast just got easier!

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ tsp baking powder (optional, but fluffier!)
  • Optional: pinch of cinnamon, vanilla, or berries

📍 How to make it:

  1. Mash banana in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs and baking powder, mix well.
  3. Heat a nonstick pan over medium.
  4. Spoon small rounds into pan.
  5. Cook 1–2 mins per side, flip gently.
  6. Stack ’em up — devour with fruit, yogurt or syrup!
