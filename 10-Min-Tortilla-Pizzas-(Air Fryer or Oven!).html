🍕 10-Min Tortilla Pizzas (Air Fryer or Oven!)

Crispy, quick, and fully customizable — pizza night just leveled up.

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:

  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • ½ cup shredded cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, olives…)

📍 How to make it:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) or air fryer to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Place tortilla on baking sheet or in fryer basket.
  3. Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, add toppings.
  4. Bake or air-fry for 5–7 minutes, until golden & bubbly.
  5. Slice and enjoy immediately!
