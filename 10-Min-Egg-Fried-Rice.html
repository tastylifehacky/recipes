🍳 10-Minute Egg Fried Rice

Leftovers never tasted so good — fast, cheap & way better than takeout!

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:

  • 1 cup cooked cold rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped vegetables (frozen peas, carrots, etc.)
  • Green onion (for garnish)

📍 How to make it:

  1. Beat the eggs and fry in a pan. Set aside.
  2. Add veggies, sauté 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  4. Return eggs, mix everything well.
  5. Garnish with green onion and serve hot!
