🥗 No-Cook Chickpea Salad (5 Minutes!)

🕐 Prep Time: 5 minutes
🍽 Servings: 2–3
🔥 Cooking: None!

✅ Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz / 400 g) chickpeas, drained & rinsed
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed preferred)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped fresh parsley or mint for garnish

🥣 Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine chickpeas, diced cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss everything gently until evenly coated.
  5. Optional: sprinkle fresh herbs on top.
  6. Serve immediately or let sit in the fridge for 10–15 min for flavors to meld.

💡 Tips:
Add feta cheese or avocado for a richer version.
Works great as a filling for pita pockets or wraps.
Best served chilled on hot days!
