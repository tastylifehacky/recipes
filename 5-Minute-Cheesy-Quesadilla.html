🧀 5-Minute Cheesy Quesadilla

Crispy outside, melty inside — all the comfort of cheese with none of the hassle.

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:

  • 1 flour tortilla
  • ½ cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a mix)
  • Optional: salsa, jalapeños, cooked chicken, herbs

📍 How to make it:

  1. Place tortilla in a hot nonstick pan.
  2. Sprinkle cheese on one half.
  3. Add optional fillings if desired.
  4. Fold tortilla over and press gently.
  5. Cook 2–3 mins per side until golden & crispy.
  6. Slice & dip — it’s cheese time!

✨ Save it for your lazy lunch days!
