🌮 Quick & Dirty Weeknight Tacos

🕒 Prep & Cook Time: ~20 minutes
🍽 Servings: 3–4
🔥 Level: Beginner (and tired 😅)

✅ Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas (flour or corn)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or plant-based alternative
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • ½ cup salsa or canned tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • Optional: sour cream, hot sauce, avocado, lime wedges

🔥 Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Brown the meat for 5–7 minutes, breaking it up as it cooks.
  3. Add spices and stir well.
  4. Add salsa or tomatoes and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, warm the tortillas.
  6. Assemble tacos: tortilla + meat + cheese + lettuce + tomato + toppings.
  7. Eat immediately — hot, juicy, and a little messy 😄

🌶️ Tips:
Swap meat for lentils or tofu.
Add a fried egg for a breakfast taco.
Want it spicier? Add chili or jalapeños.
