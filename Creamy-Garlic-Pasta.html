🍝 15-Minute Creamy Garlic Pasta

Your weeknight dinner hero — rich, garlicky, and ready before you can say "takeout"!

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:

  • 200g pasta (any kind)
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 200ml heavy cream (or milk for lighter version)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Parmesan & parsley (optional, but yum!)

📍 How to make it:

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Save ½ cup pasta water.
  2. In a pan, melt butter → sauté garlic for 1–2 mins.
  3. Add cream, salt & pepper. Simmer for 2–3 mins.
  4. Add cooked pasta + splash of pasta water. Stir well.
  5. Sprinkle cheese & herbs. Done!

✨ Save this creamy magic — your taste buds will cheer.
