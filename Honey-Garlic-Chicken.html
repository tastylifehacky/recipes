🍯 One-Pan Honey Garlic Chicken

⏱️ Ready in: 20 minutes | 🍗 Servings: 2–3 | 🧼 Easy clean-up!

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts or 4 boneless thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

🔥 Instructions:

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook chicken for 3–4 minutes per side until golden.
  3. Lower heat. Add minced garlic and stir for 30 seconds (don’t burn!).
  4. Add honey, soy sauce, and vinegar. Let it bubble for 2–3 minutes until thickened.
  5. Flip chicken in sauce until nicely glazed and fully cooked (internal temp: 165°F / 74°C).
  6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds & green onions. Serve over rice, noodles, or veggies!

😋 Tips:

  • Double the sauce if you like it extra sticky.
  • Add chili flakes for a spicy kick.
  • Works great with tofu or shrimp too!
