🥚 Microwave Omelette

Breakfast in 3 minutes — in one mug, with zero dishes!
Perfect for busy mornings, dorm life, or when your kitchen says “nope.”

👨‍🍳 You’ll need:
2 eggs, splash of milk, salt, pepper, toppings (cheese, herbs, veggies — go wild!)

📍 How to make it:

  1. Crack eggs into a mug. Add milk, salt, pepper.
  2. Stir like you mean it.
  3. Add your favorite fillings.
  4. Microwave 1 min → stir → microwave 1 more min.

Done. Fluff with a fork and eat straight from the mug!
